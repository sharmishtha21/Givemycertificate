Modern Slavery & Human Trafficking Policy

Give My Certificate is committed to preventing modern slavery and human trafficking in all our business operations and supply chains. We uphold ethical standards and respect for human rights as fundamental to our values.

Our Business:

We provide digital credentialing solutions to organizations worldwide, facilitating the issuance and verification of certificates, badges, degrees, and ID cards.

Our Policy:

Compliance: We comply with all relevant laws and regulations concerning modern slavery and human trafficking.

Risk Assessment: We conduct regular assessments to identify and mitigate risks related to modern slavery within our operations and supply chains.

Due Diligence: We implement due diligence processes for our suppliers and partners to ensure they adhere to ethical practices.

Training: We implement due diligence processes for our suppliers and partners to ensure they adhere to ethical practices.We provide training to our employees to raise awareness about modern slavery and human trafficking, ensuring they understand and can recognize the signs.

Reporting: We maintain channels for employees and stakeholders to report concerns about modern slavery, ensuring confidentiality and protection for whistleblowers.

Our Commitment:

We are dedicated to continuous improvement in our practices to combat modern slavery and human trafficking. We review our policies and procedures regularly to ensure their effectiveness.
