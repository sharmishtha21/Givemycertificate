About Us

Give My Certificate offer an advanced platform for digital certificate.
Our main aim is to digitized the certificate albite old school paper printing. We fully give assurance for the fact that it should be environment friendly as well as carry the same authenticity as the paper document. There is no need to store anywhere or no chance of being stolen. The entire process is highly secure and people can download the document when they want. While there is digitization in every aspect of our life we still use certificates printed on paper wasting precious natural resources we aspire to offer a centralized platform for the same. Institution can ask us to generate these certificates and we generate corresponding e-certificates which are cheaper, readily available, environment friendly and consume less time to make.

Trees Saved

Be a Saviour


Every year, millions of trees have been cut down to make papers of several ilks. A tree can produce roughly '9000' A4 sheets of paper. With this estimate, there will be huge amount of sacrifice of trees in the time span. As responsible people, we must avoid these circumstances by applying fully digitised certificates for us.
Providing certificates like this not only affects forests but it has an adverse effect on the environment as well. Roughly 2-13 liters of water is used to produce an A4 sheet using recovered and recycled paper the water footprint is a bit less but is still an astonishing amount reaching billions of liters of water wasted every year. So, let’s save trees before everything being wiped out.
